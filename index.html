About Finance

Finance is the study of how money is earned, managed, saved, invested, and spent by individuals, businesses, and governments.

Finance is part of daily life, but many people struggle due to lack of basic financial knowledge and language barriers. MYMONEY helps users understand finance in a simple and relatable way.

Finance involves planning, saving, spending, borrowing, and investing money to achieve financial stability and long-term growth.

Basic Financial Terms

  • Saving: Keeping money aside for future use
  • Budgeting: Planning income and expenses
  • Banking: Deposits, withdrawals, transfers
  • Digital Payments: UPI, cards, wallets
  • Investment: Using money to earn more money
  • Risk: Chance of losing money
  • Loan: Borrowed money repaid with interest

Why Finance is Important?

  • Helps manage money wisely
  • Prevents debt problems
  • Encourages saving and investing
  • Builds financial independence
  • Helps achieve long-term life goals

Financial Awareness Updates

  • Digital payments are rapidly increasing in India
  • UPI fraud cases are rising due to lack of awareness
  • Impersonation scams and fake customer care calls are increasing
  • Deepfake scams are being used for financial fraud
  • RBI warns against fake digital arrest calls
  • Saving early helps achieve financial stability
  • Understanding loans prevents debt traps

UPI & Digital Payment Safety

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) allows instant money transfer between bank accounts using mobile apps. It works 24×7 using a secure UPI PIN.

Popular UPI Platforms

  • Google Pay
  • PhonePe
  • Paytm
  • BHIM UPI

Safety Tips

  • Never share your UPI PIN or OTP
  • Banks never ask for PIN or OTP
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Do not trust unknown calls claiming to be customer care
  • Always verify payment requests
  • Use official apps only
  • Enable app lock and phone lock

Important Q&A

Q: Should you approve a payment request from an unknown number?
A: No, it could be a fraud.

Q: Does customer care ever ask for UPI PIN?
A: No, never.

Saving & Budgeting Basics

Saving means setting aside a part of your income for future needs. Budgeting means planning how money is spent to avoid unnecessary expenses.

Why Saving is Important

  • Helps during emergencies
  • Reduces financial stress
  • Builds habit of discipline
  • Supports future goals

50-30-20 Budget Rule

  • 50% – Needs (food, rent, education)
  • 30% – Wants (entertainment, shopping)
  • 20% – Savings

Steps to Create a Budget

  • Calculate monthly income
  • List fixed and variable expenses
  • Set savings goals
  • Track spending regularly

Q&A

Q: Is budgeting useful for students?
A: Yes, it helps manage pocket money and expenses.

Loan & Interest Basics

A loan is money borrowed from a bank or financial institution that must be repaid with interest.

Why People Take Loans

  • Education
  • Medical emergencies
  • Buying a house or vehicle
  • Business needs

Interest

Interest is the extra amount paid to the lender for borrowing money.

EMI

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed amount paid every month to repay a loan.

Q&A

Q: What happens if EMIs are not paid on time?
A: Penalties are charged and credit score is affected.

Banking Basics

Banks help people store money safely and provide services like withdrawals, transfers, loans, and deposits. Banks in India are regulated by RBI.

Types of Bank Accounts

  • Savings Account
  • Current Account
  • Fixed Deposit (FD)

Banking Services

  • ATM withdrawals
  • Online banking
  • Debit cards
  • Cheque facilities

Q&A

Q: Is money safe in banks?
A: Yes, banks are regulated by RBI.

Tax Basics

Tax is money paid by citizens to the government to fund public services like education, healthcare, roads, and infrastructure.

Types of Tax

  • Direct Tax: Income Tax
  • Indirect Tax: GST

Why Paying Tax is Important

  • Legal obligation
  • Supports national development
  • Avoids penalties and fines

Q&A

Q: Do students need to pay income tax?
A: Only if their income exceeds the exemption limit.
